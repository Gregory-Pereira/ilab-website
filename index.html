Let's build an open model together.

Labradorite is an open source community project based around a new language model tuning technique that enables a community of contributors to contribute knowledge upstream to a language model.

Contribute your knowledge

How it works

Labradorite is based around Large-scale Alignment for Chat Bots (LAB). LAB is a novel synthetic data-based alignment tuning method for Large Language Models (LLMs) invented by IBM Research.
